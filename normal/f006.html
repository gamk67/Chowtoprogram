Why study software?

  • Most Electrical and/or Electronic Engineering related jobs have some kind of software aspect. Many traditional Electrical Engineering functions are now performed by microprocessors.
  • If you end up in a management position you may well have to take potentially expensive decisions that are affected by your knowledge about both software and hardware.
  • Therefore, understanding and being comfortable with software will greatly enhance your career prospects.
  • Professional organisations (such as IET and IEEE) demand that you develop some software skills.
