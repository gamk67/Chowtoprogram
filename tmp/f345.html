Example: A Game of Chance

  • "Craps" dice game simulator (simplified)
  • Rules
    • Throw two dice
    • If you throw 7 or 11 on the first throw, then you win
    • If you throw 2, 3, or 12 on the first throw, then you lose
    • If you throw 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, then this becomes your "point"
    • Keep going. If you throw the point, you win. If you throw 7, you lose
