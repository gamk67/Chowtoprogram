Assignment Operators

  • Complete list of assignment operators:
    (Assume int c = 3, d = 5, e = 4, f = 6, g = 12;)

+=      c += 7      c = c + 7      10 to c

-=      d -= 4      d = d - 4      1 to d

*=      e *= 5      e = e * 5      20 to e

/=      f /= 3      f = f / 3      2 to f

%=      g %= 9      g = g % 9      3 to g
