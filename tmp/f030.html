A Simple C Program:Text output

  • printf( "Welcome to C!\n" );
  • Instructs the computer to perform an action
  • Specifically, it prints the characters within the quotes (" ")
  • The entire line is called a statement
  • All statements must end with a semicolon (;)
  • The escape character (\) indicates that printf should do something out of the ordinary
  • \n is the newline character
