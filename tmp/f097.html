The while repetition statement

  • Repetition structure
    • The programmer specifies an action to be repeated while some condition remains true.
    • Pseudocode:
      While there are more items on my shopping list
         Purchase next item and cross it off my list
    • The while loop is repeated until the condition becomes false.
  • Example:
     int product = 2;
     while ( product <= 1000 )
         product = 2 * product;
