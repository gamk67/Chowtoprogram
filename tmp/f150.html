Repetition Essentials

  • Loop
    • A group of instructions the computer executes repeatedly while some condition remains true
  • Counter-controlled repetition
    • Definite repetition: you know how many times the loop executes
    • A control variable is used to count the repetitions
  • Sentinel-controlled repetition
    • Indefinite repetition
    • Used when the number of repetitions is not known
    • The sentinel value indicates "end of data"
