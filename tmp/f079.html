Control Structures

  • Sequential execution: Statements are executed one after the other in the order written
  • Transfer of control: When the next statement executed is not the next one in sequence
  • Building blocks: All programs are written in terms of 3 control structures:
    • Sequence structures: programs are executed sequentially by default
    • Selection structures: three types: if, if…else, and switch
    • Repetition structures: three types: while, do…while and for
