Confusing Equality and Assignment

  • lvalues
    • These are expressions that can appear on the left side of an equation
    • Their values can be changed, such as variable names
    • x = 4;
  • rvalues
    • These are expressions that can appear only on the right side of an equation
    • Are constants, such as numbers
      • Cannot write 4 = x;
      • Must write x = 4;
    • lvalues can be used as rvalues, but not vice versa
      • y = x;
