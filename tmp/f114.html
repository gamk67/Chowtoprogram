Floating point numbers

  • Integer numbers have obvious limitations
    • They do not allow fractions, needed in most engineering applications
    • Integers are relatively small
  • Numbers with fractions (i.e. decimal point) are called floating point numbers, and are declared as float or double
  • Double allows larger numbers
  • They are printed with %f
  • Examples: 1.0   3.14   8.888888887   etc.
