ASCII control codes

  0  00  NUL  null
  1  01  SOH  start of heading
  2  02  STX  start of text
  3  03  ETX  end of text
  4  04  EOT  end of transmission
  5  05  ENQ  enquiry
  6  06  ACK  acknowledge
  7  07  BEL  bell
  8  08  BS   backspace
  9  09  HT   horizontal tabulation
 10  0a  LF   line feed
 11  0b  VT   vertical tabulation
 12  0c  FF   form feed
 13  0d  CR   carriage return
 14  0e  SO   shift-out
 15  0f  SI   shift-in
