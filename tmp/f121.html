Print width specification

  • Examples: 
    %f      print from current position
%20f    print at least 20 characters
%.2f    print two digits after the decimal point
%20.2f  print at least 20 characters, with two
        after the decimal point
  • If you use just %f, you often get many digits after the decimal point (typically 8 or 10) - not usually what you want.
